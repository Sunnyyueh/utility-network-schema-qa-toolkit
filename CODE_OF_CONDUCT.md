# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, technically constructive community for people of every background and experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, patient, and specific.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Welcome questions and explain project conventions.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing sensitive information.
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and correct harm when mistakes occur.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, personal attacks, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other spaces where someone represents the project. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer through the contact method on the maintainer's GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with appropriate confidentiality.

Project maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and focused on community safety.
